What is this?

Cardano gift cards are fancy paper wallets that you can gift. You can send them via email or print them and offer them in person.

How does it work?

You create a Cardano wallet and deposit the amount to its charging address. Thanks to the instructions included, the person who receives the card can learn how to retrieve their fund.

Is it secure?

Using a Cardano gift card is the least secure way to transfer cryptocurrency. We strongly recommend only moving small amounts of ADA.

How are wallets generated?

Javascript code included in this page generates the wallet on your browser. We work hard to keep our website safe; however, you should only use those wallets for small disposable amounts.

What is the safest way?

The safest way is to print a card template, write your own wallet and gift it by hand.
